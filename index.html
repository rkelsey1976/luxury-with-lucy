Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our massage services

What should I expect during my first visit?

During your first visit, you'll complete a brief health consultation form to help us understand your needs and any conditions we should be aware of. Your therapist will discuss your goals, explain the treatment, and answer any questions. You'll be given privacy to undress to your comfort level before the session begins. The entire experience is designed to make you feel relaxed and cared for.

How do I know which massage type is right for me?

Here's a quick guide:

  • Swedish Massage: Perfect for first-timers or those seeking relaxation
  • Deep Tissue: Best for chronic pain, muscle knots, or desk workers
  • Sports Massage: Ideal for athletes or active individuals
  • Hot Stone: Great for deep relaxation and muscle warming
  • Aromatherapy: Wonderful for stress relief and emotional balance
  • Prenatal: Specifically designed for expecting mothers (2nd trimester onwards)

Not sure? Contact us and we'll help you choose the perfect treatment.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 48-72 hours in advance to secure your preferred time slot, especially for weekend appointments. However, we do accommodate same-day bookings when available. Regular clients often book their next session at the end of their current appointment to ensure consistency in their wellness routine.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand plans change. Our cancellation policy is:

  • 48+ hours notice: Full refund or free rescheduling
  • 24-48 hours notice: 50% cancellation fee
  • Less than 24 hours or no-show: Full session fee charged

We always try to be flexible for emergencies – just let us know as soon as possible.

Do you offer gift vouchers?

Yes! Gift vouchers make a wonderful present for birthdays, anniversaries, Mother's Day, or just to show someone you care. They're available for specific treatments or monetary values, and are valid for 12 months from purchase. Contact us to arrange a beautifully presented voucher that can be delivered digitally or by post.

Is parking available?

Yes, there is convenient parking available nearby. We have a small private car park for clients, and there's also pay-and-display parking within a 2-minute walk. We'll provide full parking details when you book your appointment.
